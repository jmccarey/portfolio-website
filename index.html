About

Hi, my name is Joseph McCarey but I also go by the names Joey or Joe. I am an eighteen year old freshman at Harvard University but I am originally from Chicago, Illinois. This summer I am interning at Beyond Design Inc and the University of Chicago and Argonne's Array of Things project. First and foremost, however, I am a maker.

Since then I have broadened my horizon into electrical, mechanical, and software engineering along with some forms of digital and found art.
