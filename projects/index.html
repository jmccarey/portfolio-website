Animated LED Badge

A scrolling LED badge powered by an ATTINY85 microcontroller. The "n" stands for "nothing."

Completely Wireless Tripwire

An ESP8266 WiFi enabled microcontroller running off a hotspot and 9v battery. The "wire" is a laser, allowing for high range - given no obstruction.

20 Sided Die Roller Circuit

This printable circuit board uses readings of electric noise to generate random numbers between 1 & 20. The numbers are displayed using an easy to read 12 LED system.

Custom Rings

I have created numerous custom rings through chemical etching. The rings are coated in vinyl or nail polish which I carve designs into by hand. The areas carved away are exposed process and come out with an interesting etched look!
